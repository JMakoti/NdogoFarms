    About Us

    Our Problem Statement

    By 2050, over 70% of Kenya’s population will live in urban areas. This rising demand for food has led to unsafe farming practices using synthetic chemicals, harming health and the environment.

    At Ndogo Farms, we turn small urban spaces into gardens using eco-friendly grow bags and recycled containers—ensuring families access fresh, organic vegetables from their own homes.

    Our Mission

    We empower individuals to grow fresh, organic vegetables in urban spaces. By providing eco-friendly farming solutions, we aim to make sustainable farming a reality for all.

    Our Vision

    To create a future where every home, regardless of space constraints, can cultivate its own organic food, promoting sustainability and healthier communities.
